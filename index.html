Frequently Asked Questions

OET, or Occupational English Test, is an English proficiency exam designed specifically for healthcare professionals. It's important because it ensures effective communication with patients and colleagues, is often required for professional registration, and focuses on the language skills relevant to healthcare roles. This test not only boosts confidence in clinical settings but also opens up job opportunities in English-speaking countries.

The course typically lasts 8 weeks, covering all four skills: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking, with plenty of practice opportunities.

You will receive personalized feedback from expert instructors, 24/7 access to learning materials, and live webinars to help clarify your doubts. Additionally, you'll benefit from targeted practice exercises and strategies tailored to enhance your exam performance.
